# Content Checklist — answers that make this site YOURS

The structure is done; what makes a site feel real is specific, true details.
Answer these (even roughly, in WhatsApp-style notes) and they can be dropped in.
The ⭐ ones matter most — they remove the "AI-generated" feel fastest.

## 1. The teacher (this is the #1 fix) ⭐
- [ ] Teacher's full name, and how students address her (Mehak ma'am?)
- [ ] A real photo (even a good phone photo) for an "About the teacher" section
- [ ] Qualifications / certifications (degree, TEFL/TESOL, IELTS score if any)
- [ ] How many years teaching, and roughly how many students so far
      (the site currently claims "500+ students taught" and "since 2019" — both are
      placeholders; give the REAL numbers or they should be removed)
- [ ] 3–4 sentences of her story in her own words: why she teaches English,
      what she sees students struggle with — written like she talks

## 2. Facts about the business ⭐
- [ ] What year did classes actually start?
- [ ] Centre address (or at least the area/landmark in Amritsar) + Google Maps link
- [ ] Class timings (e.g. Mon–Sat, 9am–7pm) and typical session length
- [ ] How fees work (per month? per course? "discussed at demo" is fine — but say it in her words)
- [ ] Age groups accepted (youngest? adults?)
- [ ] Which languages can she explain in? (Punjabi/Hindi support is a real selling point)

## 3. The classes (replace my generic descriptions) ⭐
- [ ] In her words: what actually happens in a Spoken English class?
- [ ] What boards/grades does she take for School English? (CBSE/ICSE/PSEB?)
- [ ] IELTS: what band improvements has she genuinely seen?
- [ ] What happens in the free demo class, step by step?

## 4. Real content to plug in ⭐
- [ ] 3–9 links to YOUR YouTube videos — (the site now auto-pulls your latest
      uploads, so just make sure the channel has public videos)
- [ ] The actual PDF notes you plan to give away (files or Google Drive links + titles)
- [ ] 9 screenshots of your real Instagram posts → save as
      `public/instagram/ig1.jpg` … `ig9.jpg` to replace the placeholder photos
- [ ] 5 real FAQ questions parents/students actually ask you on WhatsApp
- [ ] First 2–3 blog topics you want to write (or say "pick for me")

## 5. Voice & positioning
- [ ] 3 words students/parents would use to describe her teaching
- [ ] One sentence: why choose her over a big coaching centre? (her answer, not marketing-speak)
- [ ] Formal English or warm Hinglish-friendly tone on the site?
- [ ] Any phrase she says all the time in class? (Great as a headline)

## 6. Contact & socials
- [ ] WhatsApp number for the forms (digits only, e.g. 91XXXXXXXXXX)
- [ ] Email address
- [ ] LinkedIn URL (footer link is ready, just needs the URL)
- [ ] Facebook page URL (same)

## 7. Practical / launch
- [ ] Domain name you want to buy (e.g. mehaklanguagelounge.com)
- [ ] Do you have a logo? (Or should the wordmark stay text-only?)
- [ ] A photo of the centre/classroom (optional, adds huge trust)
- [ ] When you get your first 2–3 real testimonials (even WhatsApp screenshots),
      send them — the hidden Reviews section can come back with real ones
